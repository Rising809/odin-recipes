Steps

  1. Into a bowl, combine beef, salt, pepper, Worcestershire sauce, soy sauce, oregano, cilantro, garlic, bell pepper and onion. Form 4 patties of 4 Â½ inches each.
  2. Heat a heavy skillet over medium heat. Then lightly toast the buns.
  3. Lightly oil the griddle and cook patties in it. Cook both the sides evenly and transfer to the buns.
  4. Into a separate bowl, combine cabbage, salt and carrot. Cook this mixture for at least 2 minutes on both sides. Divide the mixture evenly among burgers.
  5. Apply a little oil on the griddle and heat it. Sear tomato and onion until they are lightly charred. Divide among burgers.
  6. Take another bowl and combine mustard, mayonnaise and ketchup in it. Top each burger with this sauce.
